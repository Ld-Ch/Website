just a fun little site where anyone can randomly add any image anywhere

A simple image board to have fun with

A real-time, spatial canvas for collecting, and organizing, visuals.

You can drop images anywhere on an infinite-style* workspace and attach notes directly to each item. Everything stays exactly where you place it—both visually and in real time across all users.

How to use:

  1. Upload an image:

    Drag and Drop an image anywhere, or use the upload button to upload.

  2. Move image:

    Use shift + mouse click to grab your image, and move to wherever you like.

  3. Add note (optional):

    Click on the textarea and type whatever you want to go with your image.

  4. Finish!

    Click on finish editing to finalize. (Once you click finish everything is now permanant)

Key Features:

  1. Drag-and-drop visual canvas:

    Upload images from your device or drag them directly onto the board. Each item becomes an interactive “post” positioned precisely where you drop it.

  2. Spatial organization:

    Arrange content freely on a two-dimensional workspace instead of forcing it into linear lists or grids. Your layout becomes part of your thinking process.

  3. Live collaborative sync:

    Powered by a real-time backend, every change—uploads, edits, movements, and notes—is instantly synchronized across all connected users.

  4. In-place notes & editing:

    Attach and edit notes directly on each image. Double as a visual annotation tool for ideas, feedback, or context.

  5. Intelligent editing mode:

    Click into any post to lock it for editing. While active, changes are locally preserved and safely synced with debounced updates to avoid disruption.

  6. Smooth drag interactions:

    Hold Shift to reposition posts with precision. A ghost preview ensures fluid movement before final placement.

  7. Persistent storage:

    All content is stored in a backend database with image assets hosted in cloud storage, ensuring your boards remain available across sessions.

  8. Optimized rendering engine:

    A lightweight render system minimizes unnecessary re-renders using requestAnimationFrame scheduling for smooth performance, even with many items on screen.

*
